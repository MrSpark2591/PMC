What We Do

Gaurang Patel PMC® is a leading PMC firm and Architecture consultancy company, using both traditional and modular construction. We provide full turnkey building solutions using highly advanced offsite techniques. When you begin the exciting process of building your home with us, It’s all about you. Every hope. Every dream. Every day our team will rise to the challenge of making your dream home come to life. From the first sketch, to the moment you move into your new home and beyond, we remain your loyal advocates and stewards of your most precious resources – your time, budget, and peace of mind. That’s Gaurang Patel PMC®.

To manage such kind of unique projects requires an expertise with organizations and a thorough body of knowledge. Gaurang Patel Project Management Consultancy plays multifaceted part in such projects and provides the services from inception to completion of projects. At every stage of project life cycle, the principles of pro-activeness and creating the win-win situation is necessary keeping in mind the customer / client’s requirements. Use of Gaurang Patel Project Management Consultancy (PMC) offers one of the effective management solutions to increase and improve the efficiency and outcome of a project in construction.

Our team takes over everything, from an idea and concept development to realization. We believe in traditions and incorporate them within our innovations. All our projects incorporate a unique artistic image and functional solutions. Client is the soul of the project. Our main goal is to illustrate his/hers values and individuality through design.

We at MASTERS are committed to Conforming to highest standards in construction!
